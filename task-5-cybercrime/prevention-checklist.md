# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Digital scams usually work because of small mistakes. The goal is not to be paranoid, but to be aware of what actually puts you at risk.

## General Safety

- Think twice before clicking on links received through SMS, email, or messaging apps
- Do not share personal details like OTPs, passwords, or account information with anyone
- Verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks, delivery services, or support teams
- Avoid downloading apps or files from unknown or unofficial sources
- Keep your apps and phone software updated to reduce security risks

## UPI / Financial Safety

- Never enter your UPI PIN to _receive_ money - it is only required for sending money
- Do not approve payment requests unless you have initiated the transaction yourself
- Avoid scanning QR codes sent by unknown individuals, especially in buying/selling situations
- Always check the recipient’s name carefully before confirming any payment

## In Case of Fraud

- Immediately report the incident at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Contact the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 as soon as possible
- Inform your bank or payment app to block further transactions if needed
