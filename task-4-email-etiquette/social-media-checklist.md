# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

## ✅ Do’s

- Maintain a professional tone in public posts
- Think before posting anything that could affect your reputation
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your content
- Share meaningful, informative, or relevant content
- Keep your online presence consistent with your academic and professional goals

## ❌ Don’ts

- Do not share sensitive personal information publicly
- Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or controversial content
- Do not engage in arguments or toxic discussions online
- Avoid spreading unverified or misleading information
- Do not use informal or unprofessional language in public discussions
